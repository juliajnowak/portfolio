The name Hungry Hippie came from an inspiration of the classic hippie from the 1970’s in conjunction with a bakery being a place where one might be hungry.Hungry Hippie is a bakery that is based on different interpretations of the 70’s.

This concept focuses on the geometric, curved shapes of the 70’s era along with a modern take. Using type and a color scheme of muted 70’s themed colors, it utilizes curvature to create a vintage yet modern aesthetic.
This project was designed to build a brand. Utilizing a 70s theme, lines, and color scheme, Hungry Hippie was unified through the logo, icons, and products.
