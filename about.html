Hello! My name is Julia Nowak and I am currently a rising senior at Rutgers University - New Brunswick
I am on track to graduate with a BFA in Design and a minor in psychology. Over the years, I have become interested in UI/UX, Product Design, Web Design, and Branding. My Psychology minor aids my design choices by helping me grasp how the mind of potential consumers work. With design all around us, psychology also inspired me to learn how to communicate visually and have my work speak for itself.
I am skilled in the Adobe Creative Suite programs ranging from InDesign, Illustrator, to AfterEffects. To create various web design and implement UI/UX, I have also learned languages like HTML/CSS,Javascript, and worked with programs like p5.js and Glitch. Additionally, with an interest in user-friendliness, I have created applications in Figma that gave me skills in wireframing and prototyping. I have also gained insightful real-world experience while building the brand of a business I have cofounded and marketing it to investors and professionals.
When I'm not designing, I absolutely love to hike, listen to music (especially Coldplay), take photos, and dabble in visual forms of art from time to time!
